Haut-Rhin: Between Colmar and Mulhouse, the Ups and Downs of the Housing Market

The real estate market in Haut-Rhin presents a mixed picture: while some cities attract investment and new residents, others struggle with stagnation.

Published on May 29, 2019 at 08:50

“Colmar is booming. Mulhouse is trying to catch up,”

says property developer Julien Meyer. The contrast between the two cities illustrates the uneven pace of development in the region.

Colmar benefits from tourism and cultural heritage, while Mulhouse faces higher unemployment and slower growth. Infrastructural projects are underway, but recovery takes time.

Analysts note that young families still prefer affordable housing options, making Mulhouse potentially attractive — if economic stability improves.

For now, Haut-Rhin remains a patchwork of real estate realities — shaped by location, opportunity, and long-term vision.
